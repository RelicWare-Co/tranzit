## Design Context

### Users
The platform serves two primary groups:
1. **Citizens**: Users looking to schedule appointments, check requirements, and manage their reservations. They need a frictionless, mobile-friendly, and clear experience.
2. **Administrators (SIMUT)**: Staff managing schedules, capacity, overriding dates, and handling backoffice operations. They need a data-dense, reliable, and efficient desktop experience.

### Brand Personality
**Friendly, Modern, and Clear.** The tone should be approachable and helpful, removing the friction and confusion of the old manual email processes. The interface should feel official but not intimidating.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Light Mode exclusively (forced in the main app layout).
- **Color Palette**: Red as the primary brand color (inherited from the Mantine configuration), paired with crisp, high-contrast neutrals.
- **Typography**: `Geist` (sans-serif) for both headings and body, providing a clean, modern, and highly legible aesthetic.
- **Visual Tone**: Clean lines, deliberate spacing, and clear visual hierarchy. Avoid heavy gradients or generic "AI" decorations.

### Design Principles
1. **Clarity over Complexity**: For citizens, present one clear path forward at a time (e.g., OTP login, selecting a procedure).
2. **Distinct Contexts**: The citizen portal should feel airy and focused; the admin backoffice should feel organized, dense, and actionable.
3. **Optimistic & Fast**: Interactions should feel immediate, with progressive disclosure of complex information (e.g., requirements, templates).
4. **Purposeful Red Accent**: Use the primary red color strategically for primary actions and highlights, not as a blanket decoration.
